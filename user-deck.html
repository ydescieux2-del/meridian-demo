⚠️ Important: Same Device & Browser

The Four Tabs Overview

1. Campaign Command Center
See all ad campaigns (Meta & Google). Track spend, leads, CPL, CTR, ROAS. Drill-down for details.

2. CRM & Pipeline
Manage contacts. Organize by stage in kanban board. Track last contact date.

3. Nonprofit Finance
Monitor fundraising. See donor pipeline and YTD totals. Drill-down for breakdown.

4. Creative Studio
Generate presentations. Use Claude AI, Gamma, or Canva. On-demand.

Campaign Tab: View & Drill-Down

What You See

Table with: Campaign Name • Platform • Spend • Leads • CPL • CTR • ROAS

Drill-Down Feature

  1. Click any campaign name
  2. See: daily spend/leads • audience targeting • creative performance • trends

Why it matters: Spot which day campaign went flat • know which audience converts best • see lowest-CPL creative • know when to pause ads

Campaign Tab: Connect Your Data

Connect Meta Ads (Step 1)

  1. MERIDIAN → Campaign tab → "Connect Meta Ads"
  2. Redirected to Facebook
  3. Log in • Click Approve → campaigns appear

Connect Google Ads (Step 2)

  1. MERIDIAN → Campaign tab → "Connect Google Ads"
  2. Redirected to Google
  3. Log in • Click Approve → campaigns appear

Security: You never give Von your passwords. You log into Facebook/Google directly.

CRM Tab: Manage Your Contacts

Kanban Stages

New → Qualified → Proposal → Negotiation → Closed

Add a Contact

Click "+ Add Contact" • Fill in name, email, phone, company, title, tags, notes, headshot • Pick stage • Save

Move Between Stages

Drag & drop: Click contact → drag to new stage • Saved automatically

Track Follow-Ups

Use tags ("needs-call-this-week") • See "Last Contact: [date]"

Finance Tab: Tracking Grants

Add a Grant

  1. MERIDIAN → Nonprofit Finance → "+ Add Grant"
  2. Fill in: Name • Funder • Amount • Deadline • Status • Notes
  3. Save

Update Status

Find grant → click → change Status → Save

Note: Auto-reminders are Phase 2. For now, check Grant Tracker monthly.

Creative Studio: Generate Decks

Using Gamma (Most Common)

  1. Creative Studio tab → Fill in brief (topic, audience, tone, key message)
  2. Click "Generate with Gamma"
  3. Gamma opens in new tab (brief pre-filled)
  4. Click Generate → Gamma creates slides
  5. Edit in Gamma → download or share

Using Claude (Quick Outline)

Fill in brief → Click "Generate Outline" → 5-slide outline appears inline

Best Practices: Weekly Routine

  • Monday morning: Open Campaign tab • review weekend spend & leads
  • Wednesday: Check CRM • see who needs follow-ups
  • Friday: Review Nonprofit Finance • see how week's fundraising looks

Best Practices: Monthly Routine

  • First week: Export campaign performance data
  • Second week: Review donor pipeline • move donors to next stage
  • Third week: Check grant tracker for upcoming deadlines
  • Fourth week: Build monthly board report using MERIDIAN data

Data Entry Tips

  • When adding contact: always include note on where you met them
  • Use consistent tags (don't mix "hot-prospect" and "priority")
  • Update "Last Contact" date after every conversation
  • Upload professional headshot (helps you remember faces)
  • Check MERIDIAN weekly instead of ad platforms daily

Troubleshooting FAQ

Q: I lost all my data!
A: You cleared browser cache or switched devices. Until Phase 2, always use same browser/device.

Q: Campaign numbers look wrong.
A: Data updates every 5 minutes. Wait 10 min and refresh. If still wrong, check Meta Ads Manager.

Q: No grant deadline reminder.
A: Auto-reminders are Phase 2. Check Grant Tracker monthly yourself.

Q: Can I use MERIDIAN on iPhone?
A: Yes, it's mobile-friendly! But data won't sync to Mac until Phase 2.

Security: How Your Data Is Protected

✓ OAuth 2.0: You log into Meta/Google/QB directly — not through MERIDIAN
✓ Secure Connections: All data sent over HTTPS (locked)
✓ Data on Your Device: Phase 1 stores data only on your computer (localStorage)
✓ Plaid Security: Bank login goes directly to your bank — Von never sees it

Security: Phase 2 Enhancements

✓ Database Encryption: Sensitive info encrypted in database
✓ Authentication: Email + password or login via Google/Microsoft
✓ Audit Logs: Full record of who accessed/changed what and when
✓ Automatic Backups: Your data backed up automatically — never lose it
✓ Quarterly Key Rotation: API keys refreshed automatically
