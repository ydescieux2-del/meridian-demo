User Guide for DNA Agency

Getting Started, Daily Use, Account Setup

For Nicole Feenstra and the DNA Agency team

Welcome to MERIDIAN

Your private operations dashboard that brings together:

  • 📊 Campaigns — All your ad spend and performance
  • 👥 Contacts — Prospects, partners, everyone in a pipeline
  • 💰 Fundraising — Donor pipeline and grant tracking
  • 🎨 Creative — AI-powered deck generation

How to Access

  1. Bookmark: https://ydescieux2-del.github.io/meridian-demo/
  2. Open in your browser
  3. No login, no installation needed

⚠️ Important: Same Device & Browser

⚠️ IMPORTANT (Until Phase 2)
Your data lives on the device you use. If you open MERIDIAN in Chrome on your Mac, your data is stored there. If you switch to Safari or a different computer, you'll see an empty dashboard.

Best Practice

  • ✓ DO: Always open MERIDIAN in the same browser on the same device
  • ✗ DON'T: Switch between browsers or computers
  • ✗ DON'T: Open MERIDIAN in incognito/private mode (data won't be saved)

We'll upgrade this in Phase 2 so your data syncs everywhere.

The Four Tabs: Quick Overview

1. Campaign Command Center
See all your ad campaigns (Meta & Google) in one place. Track spend, leads, CPL, CTR, ROAS.

2. CRM & Pipeline
Manage all your contacts, prospects, and partners. Organize them by stage in a kanban board.

3. Nonprofit Finance
Monitor fundraising and grants. See your donor pipeline and YTD totals.

4. Creative Studio
Generate presentations on-demand using Claude AI, Gamma, and Canva.

Tab 1: Campaign Command Center

What You See

A table showing each active campaign with:

  • Campaign Name
  • Platform (Meta or Google)
  • Total Spend
  • Leads Generated
  • Cost Per Lead (CPL) — lower is better
  • Click-Through Rate (CTR)
  • Return on Ad Spend (ROAS)

Campaign Tab: Drill-Down Feature

How It Works

  1. Click any campaign name
  2. See detailed breakdown including:
    • Daily spend and leads (calendar view)
    • Audience targeting details
    • Creative ad performance
    • Performance trends over time

Why This Matters

Instead of just "Campaign spent $5,000 and got 150 leads":

  • See which day the campaign went flat
  • Know which audience segment converts best
  • Identify which ad creative has the lowest CPL
  • Know when to pause underperforming ads

Campaign Tab: Connect Your Meta Ads

Steps

  1. Go to MERIDIAN → Campaign Command Center tab
  2. Look for "Connect Meta Ads" button
  3. Click it → Redirected to Facebook
  4. Log in with your Facebook account
  5. Click Approve when asked for permission
  6. Redirect back to MERIDIAN
  7. Your campaigns appear after a few seconds
Security: You never give Von your Facebook password. MERIDIAN just asks Facebook for permission to read your campaigns.

Campaign Tab: Connect Your Google Ads

Steps

  1. Go to MERIDIAN → Campaign Command Center tab
  2. Look for "Connect Google Ads" button
  3. Click it → Redirected to Google
  4. Log in with your Google account
  5. Click Approve
  6. Redirect back to MERIDIAN
  7. Google campaigns appear in your dashboard

Troubleshooting

Can't see a Connect button? Refresh the page.

Invalid Account error? You need admin access to the ad account.

Spend numbers look wrong? Wait 10 minutes for data to sync.

Tab 2: CRM & Pipeline

What You See

A kanban-style board with contact lanes:

  • New — Leads you just acquired
  • Qualified — Good fit, interest confirmed
  • Proposal — You've sent a proposal
  • Negotiation — Back-and-forth, getting closer
  • Closed — Deal won or lost

What You Can Do

Add contacts, drag-drop between stages, edit details, upload headshots, add notes and tags

CRM Tab: Adding a Contact

Steps

  1. Go to MERIDIAN → CRM & Pipeline
  2. Click "+ Add Contact"
  3. Fill in the form:
    • Name (required)
    • Email, Phone, Company, Title
    • Tags (e.g., "hot-prospect", "major-donor")
    • Notes (e.g., "Met at March gala")
    • Social links (LinkedIn, Twitter, etc.)
    • Headshot (upload a photo)
  4. Pick a Stage
  5. Click Save

CRM Tab: Managing Your Pipeline

Moving Contacts Between Stages

  • Drag & Drop: Find contact → drag to new stage → saved automatically
  • Or Edit: Click contact → change Stage dropdown → Save

Using Tags for Priorities

  • Tag contacts: "needs-call-this-week", "waiting-for-them"
  • Filter by tag to see all tagged contacts
  • Work through them systematically

Tracking Last Contact

Each contact shows "Last Contact: [date]". If it says "45 days ago" → time to reach out!

Tab 3: Nonprofit Finance

What You See

Four main sections:

  • Donor Pipeline: Visual funnel (Education → Engagement → Ask → Thank)
  • Donor Records: Individual profiles with gift history
  • Grant Tracker: Grants with deadlines, status, amounts
  • YTD Totals: Where your money came from this year

Finance Tab: Donor Pipeline

The Four Stages

Stage What It Means When to Move
Education Prospects who know about you After initial contact
Engagement They're engaged (attended event, opened emails) Once they RSVP or respond
Ask You've made a funding request After you pitch/ask for money
Thank Donors who've given Once they contribute

Why This Matters: See conversion rates. Are lots in Education but few moving to Ask? That's your signal to focus on engagement.

Finance Tab: YTD Totals & Drill-Down

What the Dashboard Shows

  • Total gifts received this year (by month)
  • Number of donors
  • Average gift size
  • Grant awards received

Drill-Down Example

Dashboard shows: "$127,000 raised in March"

Click on March → see breakdown:

  • $25,000 gift from Alice Chen
  • $15,000 matching gift from her company
  • $60,000 awarded from May Grant
  • Plus several smaller gifts

Finance Tab: Tracking Grants

Adding a Grant

  1. Go to MERIDIAN → Nonprofit Finance tab
  2. Click "+ Add Grant"
  3. Fill in:
    • Grant Name (e.g., "CDC Summer Youth Program")
    • Funder (e.g., "Gates Foundation")
    • Amount ($250,000)
    • Deadline (date)
    • Status (Applied, Awarded, Pending, Rejected)
    • Notes
  4. Click Save

Updating Status

Find grant → click to edit → change Status → Save

Tab 4: Creative Studio

Generating a Deck with Gamma (Most Common)

  1. Go to MERIDIAN → Creative Studio tab
  2. Fill in your brief:
    • Deck Topic (e.g., "Q1 2026 Campaign Performance")
    • Audience (e.g., "DNA Agency Board")
    • Tone (e.g., "Professional but optimistic")
    • Key Message
    • Number of Slides
  3. Click "Generate with Gamma"
  4. Gamma opens in new tab with your brief pre-filled
  5. Click Generate inside Gamma → AI creates slides
  6. Edit/refine in Gamma → download or share

Creative Studio: Quick Outlines

Generating an Outline with Claude (MERIDIAN AI)

  1. Fill in your brief form
  2. Click "Generate Outline (Claude)"
  3. A 5-slide outline appears inline (no new tab)
  4. Example output:
    • Slide 1: Q1 Performance Dashboard
    • Slide 2: Channel Breakdown
    • ...etc
  5. Copy outline → paste into Gamma or edit further

Best Practices

Weekly Routine

  1. Monday morning: Open Campaign tab, review weekend spend and leads
  2. Wednesday: Check CRM, see who needs follow-ups
  3. Friday: Review Nonprofit Finance, see how week's fundraising looks

Monthly Routine

  1. First week: Export campaign performance data
  2. Second week: Review donor pipeline, move donors to next stage
  3. Third week: Check grant tracker for upcoming deadlines
  4. Fourth week: Build monthly board report using MERIDIAN data

Data Entry Best Practices

  • When adding a contact, always include a note about how/where you met them
  • Use consistent tags (don't mix "hot-prospect" and "priority")
  • Update "Last Contact" date every time you talk to someone
  • Upload a professional headshot (helps you remember faces)
  • Check MERIDIAN weekly instead of ad platforms daily (less context switching)

Common Questions & Troubleshooting

Q: I lost all my data!
A: You probably cleared your browser cache or switched devices. Until Phase 2, always use the same browser/device. Ask Von to help restore if you have a backup.

Q: Campaign numbers look wrong.
A: Data updates every 5 minutes. Wait 10 min and refresh. If still wrong, check Meta Ads Manager/Google Ads to confirm.

Q: Grant deadline reminder didn't send.
A: Auto-reminders are Phase 2. For now, check your Grant Tracker monthly.

Q: Can I use MERIDIAN on my iPhone?
A: Yes, it's mobile-friendly! But data on iPhone won't sync to Mac until Phase 2.
