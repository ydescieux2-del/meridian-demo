Why Phase II
Phase I gave you the operating system. Phase II makes it intelligent. Live integrations that sync automatically, AI-powered proposals, client-facing reports, and team collaboration tools — so Meridian runs your business instead of just tracking it.
Feature 01
Live QuickBooks + Bank Sync
Real-time financial data — no manual entry, no switching tools
QuickBooks OAuth
One-click sign-in to your QuickBooks account. Invoices, payments, and expenses auto-sync daily into Meridian's Finance tab — no manual entry, always current.
Plaid Bank Linking
Secure bank and credit card connections via Plaid. Every transaction auto-categorized and visible in your dashboard — checking, savings, and credit cards in one view.
Auto-Reconciliation
QB invoices matched to bank deposits automatically. Meridian flags discrepancies and unmatched transactions so nothing slips through the cracks.
Feature 02
AI-Powered Proposals
Generate client proposals in your brand voice in under 60 seconds
Brand Voice AI
Trained on your past proposals and DNA Agency's tone. Select a project, pick the tone (witty, sharp, professional, warm), and generate a polished proposal draft instantly.
Auto-Populate from Projects
Pulls deliverables, timelines, and team assignments directly from your Operations tab. No copy-pasting scope details — the proposal builds itself from your project data.
PDF Export + Version History
Export branded PDFs ready to send. Edit before exporting. Every version saved so you can track what was sent to whom and when.
Feature 03
Client Reporting + Exports
Professional reports your clients actually want to read
Campaign Performance Reports
One-click PDF reports showing campaign KPIs, deliverables completed, spend vs. budget, and ROI — branded with DNA Agency's look. Send to clients in seconds.
Financial Exports
PDF and CSV exports of P&L, expense breakdowns, and contractor cost summaries — formatted for your bookkeeper or accountant. No more reformatting spreadsheets.
Scheduled Delivery
Set it and forget it. Auto-generate and email monthly reports to clients on a schedule. Keeps clients informed without manual effort.
Feature 04
Team Notifications + Slack Integration
Keep your team in sync without chasing people
Slack / Google Chat Webhooks
Connect Meridian to Slack or Google Chat. Campaign status changes, assignment updates, and deadline alerts automatically post to your team channel.
In-App Notifications
Activity feed inside Meridian showing who changed what, when. Campaign reassignments, status updates, new time entries — all visible at a glance in Operations.
Multi-User Access
Role-based logins for your team. Contractors see their hours and assignments. You see everything. Each person gets the view they need — nothing more, nothing less.
